The Free Time Database: Philadelphia

A database for field trip ideas, in-person or virtually!

What is this?

This is a database designed to help parents and caregivers find fun, cost-efficient, and accessible activities in the city of Philadelphia. Parents/Caregivers will begin by taking a simple survey. Based on their answers, the database will show three options for children in a given age range.

How can I see more?

To access all of the options, please create an account. Parents and Caregivers can also leave comments, tips, and recommendations for places that they visit!
